Events

CSI M.ART.IN brings to you a variety of technical and non-technical events. Read on to learn more!

Dazzle Coder

A coding event where the participant is asked to solve a problem although his vision is blinded.

Morsquode

A technical event where teams of four need to solve the given puzzles in the form of a computer code along with the help of the international Morse code.

Puzzle Trail

A series of coding puzzles where the answer to every puzzle combines to form another puzzle. The winner is decided by the person closest to the solution

Fix-It Felix

A debugging event based on a famous arcade game where the most number of issues resolved earns the highest score.

Code Blooded

An offline programming quiz including questions of theoretical and syntactical nature

Hackathon

A coding event to complete the given incomplete programs, keeping in mind the practicality and significance in real-world problems.

Pew_Die_Py

A Python-exclusive coding contest which involves famous YouTube channels as the theme.

Code Painter

A Java-exclusive designing contest where the participants draw a picture with the given limited set of commands.

Beyond Marvel and DC

A quiz game where contestants are tested of their knowledge about the fictional universes of Marvel and DC.

The One With F.R.I.E.N.D.S

A multi-round paired quiz event based on a popular TV series, where a hidden scoreboard decides the winners.

Find The End

An event which tests the participants' memory to the fullest. Initially, the participants are shown a pattern to remember. The participants then have to traverse through a darkened room according to the pattern. The participant who goes the furthest in the least amount of time wins.

Guess The Character

A team event to find five unique objects in the campus, take a selfie with it and submit it to the organizers. The team which finds all the objects in a minimum amount of time wins

Friendship Compatibility

A team event game for a pair of students, one would be reading something very loud and the other one should understand their lip movements while listening to loud music.

Short film contest

An online event where the participant must create a short film which will be judged on its creativity.

Taki Taki

An online event to make the best TikTok video.

Infinity Theories

An online event where the participants submit their fan theories regarding the Endgame

Minute to Win it

A series of short challenges where the participant has only one minute to win the game. ● Balancing coins on a pencil ● Paper toss against a fan ● 1 Bottle, 1 Cup
